Work, Power, Energy

Work
Nov 11

Work is product of force consumed for certain distance. In some places where mass is in Kg we need to use mgh formula. This helps in converting mass into force, here m is mass, g is acceleration due to gravity and h is height or distance covered. When force is directly specified then we use force X displacement formula.

Work

m = mass

g = acceleration due to gravity

h, s = displacement

f = force applied

  • w = mgh
  • w = f*s

    Power
    Nov 11

    Power is ratio of work done with time. It is also equal to force times displacement.

    Power

    w = work

    t = time consumed

    v = velocity

    f = force applied

  • p = w/t
  • p = f*v
